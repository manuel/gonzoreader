privacy

I don't keep any logs; however 110mb.com and Google may monitor your use of Gonzo Reader, and Disqus may log your visits to this page.

public domain

Gonzo Reader is in the public domain. Just copy the source files, or check them out on GitHub.

"Leave Gonzo Reader?" alert

Some pages try to break out of the content frame. In this case, Gonzo Reader will ask you two times whether you want to leave. Just click cancel two times. (The dialog appears two times because Gonzo Reader prefetches the next page for quicker loading.)

browsers

So far, I have only tested it in Firefox 3.
